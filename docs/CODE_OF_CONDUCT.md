# Code of Conduct

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10

Our pledge for maintaining a welcoming and inclusive community.

---

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

- **Respect differing viewpoints and experiences**
- **Gracefully accept constructive criticism**
- **Focus on what is best for the community**
- **Show empathy towards other community members**

### Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment**: Sexual language or imagery, unwelcome sexual attention, trolling, insulting/derogatory comments
- **Exclude**: Discriminatory jokes, language, or content
- **Personal attacks**: Political, religious, or other directed attacks

## Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers

- **Clarify standards** of acceptable behavior
- **Take appropriate action** in response to any incidents
- **Be fair** when applying these standards

### Community Members

- **Report violations** to maintainers
- **Be respectful** when addressing others
- **Follow these guidelines** in all interactions

## Incident Reporting

### How to Report

1. Email: security@skill-split.dev
2. GitHub: Report via "Report abuse" feature
3. Direct message: Contact any maintainer

### What to Include

- Your contact information
- Description of incident
- Timestamp/location
- Any relevant screenshots

## Enforcement

### Consequences

- **Warning** for first offenses
- **Temporary ban** for repeated offenses
- **Permanent ban** for severe violations

### Appeals

Contact: security@skill-split.dev
